New Stars and Demos

Below we list the new stars and demos by domain.
  • HOFBusInterleave3 star - Tom Lane
  • SunVideo star, which uses the Sun Video camera as a source and the SDFTkPicture.pl star, which Display a greyscale image on a Tk widget - Luis Gutierrez

    Demos:

  • Sun Video Demonstration
  • Sun Video Edge Detect
    • (The SunVideo star and demos will only work on a Sun workstation with a Sun Video card) - Luis Gutierrez
  • NdftVarD star that implements a Nonuniform Discrete Fourier Transform (NDFT) - Matt Felder, Jimmy Mason (UT Austin)
  • 60Ghz DE Demo - Jens Voigt
  • SDFReadAIFF and SDFPlayAIFF stars that read and play AIFF Audio files on SGI machines - Charles B. Owen
  • SDF and CGC neural network stars, galaxies, and demos - Biao Lu and Brian Evans
  • SDF Neural Network stars
  • SDF Neural Network Demonstrations
  • CGC Neural Network Demonstrations
  • SDF smart antennas demonstration using the constant modulus algorithm - Srikanth Gummadi and Brian Evans
  • SDF galaxies ITUDTMFGenerator and ITUDTMFplusNoiseGenerator for testing Dual-Tone Multi-Frequency (touchtone) detectors for ITU compliance - Matt Felder, Jimmy Mason, and Brian Evans
  • The icons for these galaxies can be found in the SDF Sources Palette
  • ITUDTMFGenerator Galaxy
  • ITUDTMFplusNoiseGenerator Galaxy
  • Two Person FSM Reflex Game - Bilung Lee
