Sun Audio

TychoTarget Palette The Tycho Audio Demos only work under Solaris. Some of the TychoTarget demos process audio in realtime. If you have a CD Player on your Sparcstation, then you can use /usr/openwin/bin/audiotool and workman to play audio CDs through the TychoTarget apps.

Workman

ftp.hyperion.com contains the source files for workman. Under Solaris2.x workman can be set up to play audio CD's automatically upon insertion.

Using the CDROM as a source

Only the Sparc5 and a few obscure Sparc10s can get audio from the CD directly.

Most other Sparcs can use a mini jump plug from the headphone jack on the cdrom to the line in on the back of the machine

You can then use /usr/openwin/bin/audiotool to control the inputs and outputs. Look under the Volume menu button.
