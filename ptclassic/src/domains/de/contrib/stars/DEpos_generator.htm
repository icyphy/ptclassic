pos_generator star in DE domain

This star generates a uniformly distributed position in the area (x_min, y_min) (x_max, y_max). The position is returned as a complex value, where x is the real part and y is the imag. part of the complex particle.

Derived from: DEStar
Location: The WiNeS-Library
Version: 1.1 "06/24/97"
Author: A. Wernicke

Inputs

restartint

Outputs

R_PosComplex

States

x_maxIntState "5000"
y_minIntState "50"
y_maxIntState "2600"
