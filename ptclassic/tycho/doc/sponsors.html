Sponsors of the Tycho Project

  • The Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA),
  • The State of California MICRO program,

and the following companies:

  • The Alta Group of Cadence Design Systems,
  • Dolby Laboratories,
  • Hewlett Packard,
  • Hitachi,
  • LG Electronics,
  • Lockheed Martin ATL,
  • NEC,
  • Philips, and
  • Rockwell
