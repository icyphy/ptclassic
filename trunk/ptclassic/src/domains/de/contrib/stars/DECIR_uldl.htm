CIR_uldl star in DE domain

The interfences are calculated here by calling the appropriate methods in the propagation-star. This star is called each time a mobile station appears in the environment or one mobile station vanishes from the environment. Both events of that kind cause a new interference situation which is then calculated. Information and data are given out to animation and data analysis stars.
