Mobile_Station star in DE domain

That star receives the demands for creating a new mobile station from the activation. It checks the position and places the mobile in the right cell. If the the received position is not inside of one of the rooms, it demands a new position rather than dropping that mobile away.

Derived from: DEcell_list
Location: The WiNeS-Library
Version: 1.3 "12/08/97"
Author: A. Wernicke, J. Voigt

Inputs

R_PosComplex
startint

Outputs

restartint
Rx_PosComplex
cellnumint
bandwint
